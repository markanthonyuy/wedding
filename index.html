The Planning

After the proposal, I'm not ready for what's coming. Wedding planning is NOT EASY. Let me give you a moment to sink that in. Paperworks, Venue and Church occulars, then you have to think of your Guest, Sponsors, Entourage, Finances (make sure you have the means to get this done and stick to your budget.) and countless of wedding expo that we attended and boy oh boy it was a handful. Did you know that I don't like crowds? Groups are OK but then I consider 5 people is a group and more than that is a crowd so "meh".

It was quite a while when we found the Venue, Church, Photographer, Videographer and other suppliers that we want. But is so well worth it in the end. Trust the process!

I'm planning to make a list of that by the way. If your interested, just let me know. LOL

This all happened even if though we still haven't decided yet what is the exact date of our wedding day. It was June or July 2019 that we finally pick October 10 to be our wedding day. And yes, you read that right October 10 2019 was the original date of our wedding.

The reason it was moved to October 4 2019 is because of the venue that we want is not available on October 10 but one of the hidden reason for me is that October 4 is also my wife's birthday so it's double whammy for me. 1 gift for two occasions and for future reasons that I won't be able to forget 1 of those occasions. Kapaw!
